How to add Sell It Platform Quick Widget

  1. From your Home screen in android phone, touch and hold an empty space.
  2. Tap Widgets which is located at the bottom of your screen.
  3. Search for Sell It Platform which looks like this:



  4. Touch and hold Sell It Platform widget and add to home screen.
  5. Drag the widget to the preferred Home screen then release.
    (For the widget to be successfully added, there must be sufficient space on the
    screen.)
